# Glossary (KB sample)

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **RPU** | Rollup processing unit: one scheduled job slice that processes a bounded partition of events into hourly aggregates. |
| **Northline** | Internal codename for the metrics pipeline described in `01_product_brief.md`. |
| **Reconciliation window** | UTC period when batch jobs may rewrite aggregate tables; see product brief for hours. |

## Note

If product brief and glossary disagree on numbers, **product brief wins** for SLA numerics; glossary wins for pure terminology.
